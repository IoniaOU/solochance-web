Privacy Policy

Last updated March 2026

1. Overview

SoloChance.org does not collect, store, or sell any personal data. No account or registration is required to use the service.

2. Calculator API Requests

When you perform a calculation, your selected currency, hashrate value, and unit are sent to our API at api.solochance.org to retrieve live network data. No IP address or other identifying information is stored beyond standard server access logs, which are retained for a short period for operational purposes only.

3. Google AdSense

This site displays advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalised advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

4. YouTube Embed

A YouTube video is embedded on the main page. YouTube may set cookies when you interact with the player. This is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Cookies

SoloChance.org itself does not set any first-party cookies. Cookies may be set by third-party services (Google AdSense, YouTube) as described above.

6. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
